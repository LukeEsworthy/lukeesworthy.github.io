About Me

Hello! I'm Luke Esworthy

full-stack web developer || world traveler || car fanatic


As a current student at Nashville Software School and a perpetual student of life, I greatly value any chance to learn and grow. Whether doing something new or for the hundredth time, there is always an opportunity to get something new from every experience.

After graduating with my B.S. in Business Administration from The University of Tennessee at Knoxville, I continued working in the automotive industry, where my passion is. In that time, I have been able to try my hand at many facets of the industry: photographing inventory and managing online sales site & advertisements, selling, managing a lot of vehicles for sale, completing regular maintenance & repairs, and custom performance enhancements. I have enjoyed the challenges I faced, the knowledge I gained and the relationships I made.

My decision to make the move into being a full-time student and a complete shift in industries came not from boredom but from a change in where I see my life going, and this is my first step in that direction.

Outside of school and work, I like sunsets, long walks on the beach... alright, I'm being a little facetious, but at the same time I truly do love a beautiful sunset. One of my biggest passions is traveling, and I have been fortunate enough to have watched some amazing sunsets in some amazing places. Experiencing other cultures and exploring the food, history, and traditions of other places is something that I do not think I could get tired of even if I visited every country in the world--but I would love to put that to the test!

In addition to traveling, I have a passion for cars. My love for them is what lead me to my career in the automotive industry, and is a big part of my continuous drive (pun fully intended) to adapt and grow not just personally but professionally as well, so that I can pursue what has turned out to be a pretty expensive obsession!
